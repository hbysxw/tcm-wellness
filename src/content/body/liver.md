## The Liver in TCM vs. Western Medicine

<div class="my-8 rounded-xl overflow-hidden shadow-md">

The Western Liver detoxifies. The TCM Liver does that — but its primary job is to **keep Qi flowing smoothly** throughout the entire body. When the Liver is happy, everything flows. When it's stuck, problems cascade.

**Key functions:**
- 🌊 Ensures smooth flow of Qi
- 🩸 Stores the Blood during rest
- 👁️ Opens into the eyes
- 😤 Connected to anger and frustration
