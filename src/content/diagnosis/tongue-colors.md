## Practical Self-Check

<div class="bg-amber-50 border border-amber-200 rounded-lg p-4 my-6">
  <h4 class="font-semibold text-sm text-amber-800 mb-2">How to assess tongue color accurately</h4>
  <ul class="text-sm text-amber-700 space-y-1">
    <li>• Use <strong>natural daylight</strong> — fluorescent lights distort colors</li>
    <li>• Look at the <strong>body color</strong>, not the coating</li>
    <li>• Compare to the <strong>inside of your cheek</strong> — that is your normal pink baseline</li>
    <li>• Check <strong>consistently</strong> — at the same time of day, before eating</li>
  </ul>
</div>
